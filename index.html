DON'T STOP MOVING THE SQUARE!

WARNING. THIS GAME CONTAINS IMAGES THAT MAY APPEAR DISTURBING. DISCRETION IS ADVISED.

How to play:

Use the mouse cursor to click anywhere on the game screen.

Once the square starts moving, click again anywhere of the screen before the square reaches where you clicked before.

Each click gives you 10 points and speeds up the square.

You win once you get 1000.
